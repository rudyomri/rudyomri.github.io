About the web map...

This web map visualizes the historical timeline of present University of Oregon campus buildings from 1875 to present. Note that past buildings that have been demolished are not included in this map. You can animate the map by clicking on the VCR buttons or by dragging the timeline slider. You may click on any campus building to learn more about its history. For more in-depth details, visit the building index page.

Developed by Rudy Omri. Dept of Geography. University of Oregon. 2015.
